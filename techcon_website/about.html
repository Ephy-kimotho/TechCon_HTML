Brief History of TechCon

TechCon, short for Technology Conference.
It is an annual event that has grown to become a premier gathering for tech enthusiasts, industry leaders, and innovators from around the globe.

Origins

  • InceptionTechCon was first held in 2010, starting as a small meetup for tech professionals to share their knowledge and discuss emerging trends.
  • Founding VisionThe primary goal was to create a platform where technology experts and enthusiasts could come together to network, learn, and showcase their innovations

Evolution

  • 2015Introduced dedicated tracks for emerging technologies like AI and IoT.
  • 2017Launched the startup pitch competition, providing a platform for budding entrepreneurs to present their ideas to potential investors.
  • 2019Achieved a record attendance of over 10,000 participants, featuring renowned speakers from top tech companies and academia.
  • 2020Adapted to a virtual format due to the COVID-19 pandemic, allowing even wider global participation and setting new standards for virtual conferencing.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and inspiration within the global technology community.

By providing a premier platform for tech enthusiasts, professionals, and industry leaders, TechCon aims to:

  • Facilitate the exchange of cutting-edge ideas, research, and developments across various technology domains.
  • Inspire participants to push the boundaries of technology through exposure to the latest trends, tools, and practices.
  • Create opportunities for meaningful connections and collaborations among attendees, including developers, entrepreneurs, investors, and thought leaders.
  • Offer a platform for startups and emerging tech companies to showcase their innovations, gain visibility, and connect with potential investors and partners.
